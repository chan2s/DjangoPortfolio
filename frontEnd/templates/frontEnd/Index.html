01About Me

I build things for the web and beyond.

Hi there! I'm Cristian Paul Guillen, a 3rd-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Negros Oriental State University. I grew up in Villareal, Bayawan City — a small town that taught me resourcefulness and a love for problem-solving.

My journey into tech started in high school, driven by curiosity about how digital things work. Today I build web applications for school projects and continuously expand my skill set. I'm currently deepening my knowledge in Python while honing my existing skills in PHP, Java, and front-end technologies.

I believe great software is built with both technical skill and creative thinking. I'm eager to contribute to real-world projects and grow as a developer.
